# SkillsUp Platform

> Empowering Blue-Collar Workers with Job Opportunities and Skills Development

## üéØ Mission

SkillsUp Platform is a comprehensive hub designed to connect blue-collar workers with meaningful employment opportunities while providing accessible pathways to learn new skills and advance their careers. We bridge the gap between employers seeking skilled workers and individuals looking to build successful careers in trades and skilled professions.

## ‚ú® Features

### For Job Seekers

- **Job Discovery**: Browse and search for blue-collar job opportunities across various industries
  - Construction, Manufacturing, Logistics, Maintenance, Hospitality, and more
  - Filter by location, experience level, salary range, and job type
  - Save favorite jobs and set up job alerts

- **Skills Training & Development**
  - Access free and premium courses on essential trade skills
  - Video tutorials, interactive lessons, and hands-on projects
  - Certifications and credentials recognized by industry partners
  - Skill assessments to identify strengths and areas for improvement

- **Career Profile**
  - Create comprehensive profiles showcasing skills, certifications, and experience
  - Digital portfolio for work samples and project documentation
  - Track your learning progress and completed certifications
  - Resume builder tailored for blue-collar professions

- **Learning Pathways**
  - Structured learning paths for specific trades (electrician, plumber, welder, etc.)
  - Apprenticeship and mentorship opportunities
  - Industry-recognized certification preparation
  - Soft skills development (safety, communication, teamwork)

### For Employers

- **Talent Discovery**: Find qualified blue-collar workers with verified skills
- **Job Posting**: Easy-to-use interface for posting job opportunities
- **Skills Matching**: AI-powered matching with candidates based on required skills
- **Training Partnership**: Partner with the platform to provide custom training programs

## üìñ Usage

### For Job Seekers

1. **Sign Up**: Create a free account with your email
2. **Build Your Profile**: Add your skills, experience, and certifications
3. **Browse Jobs**: Search and apply for jobs that match your skills
4. **Start Learning**: Enroll in courses to develop new skills
5. **Earn Certifications**: Complete courses and assessments to earn credentials
6. **Track Progress**: Monitor your learning journey and job applications

### For Employers

1. **Create Employer Account**: Register as an employer or company
2. **Post Jobs**: Create detailed job listings with requirements
3. **Review Candidates**: Browse qualified candidates who match your needs
4. **Connect**: Reach out to potential hires directly through the platform
